Tackling the Crude Imperfections

Event Overview

This workshop is going to provide you with a faulty print and Dave Evans will walk you through the process he uses at Ballard Power to quickly polish his prints. This workshop will show the various methods for working with ABS, PLA, and VeroBlack/VeroWhite as materials. You will experience how to make a NaOH bath, effectively sand, and proper painting techniques for applying a primer and varnish. This will also be a safe place to try repairing incomplete layers using a razor blade.
