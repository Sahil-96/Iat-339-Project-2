Printed Coffee

Sit, Sip, and Socialize as you Design

"We’re always looking ahead of the curve. We do this by seeing the bigger picture and making the tools we need right now." - Founder Jordan Dawson

What We Offer

There is an innovator in everyone, so we work to help you bring your ideas to life. We offer a 24 hour support line to help troubleshoot and provide advice on any Makerbot software issues. Every location has at least 12 work stations for public use that are connected to 3d printers. For the last 18 years we have been hosting weekly competitions, seminars, and showcases.

Our Vibe

At our core, Printed Coffee operates on Imagination, Enginuity, and Communication. We want to hear how your doing and what your up to, and make it better. You can come in plug in your headphones and work until your beanie falls off, or collaborate with a team trying to find the best way to recreate Rhaegal. Our bustling locations, always have something going on, and something new on display.

Our Work Stations

Our work stations are fully equipped with 2tb of storage, 12 core processors, and RTX 2080Ti’s. That means, our work stations are like battle stations. You can design with minimal hardware hiccups, and work for hours on end. Each station is equipped with all 3D printer softwares as well as some popular modelling programs like Maya, and Blender.

In-store Guidance for all things 3D

Each of our locations is home to the professional Build Masters team. They are comprised of experts that provide an unmatched level of support regarding all things 3D. Their experience range from repairing broken printers, to helping resolve issues in your 3D design you intend to print. They provide 24/7 support online, over the phone, in store, or even travel on site with an appointment.

Bring your Designs to life!

Printed Coffee has had the largest collection of 3D printers and filaments on site for the last 10 years. Our policy is that if you need a design printed, there will always be a workstation and printer available for you to print with! We have provided over 3.4 Million prints in store since our opening. Now the question is, are you going to help us keep our Guinness world record and bring your designs to life?
